Banaras Locomotive Summary

Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW) is a leading locomotive manufacturer in India, known for its production of diesel and electric locomotives. BLW is located in Varanasi (Banaras), Uttar Pradesh, and plays a crucial role in supplying locomotives to Indian Railways. The locomotives manufactured by BLW are integral to the efficient operation of freight and passenger trains across India's vast railway network. BLW continues to innovate and expand its capabilities in locomotive manufacturing.
